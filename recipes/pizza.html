Pizza

Description

This recipe will show you how to create a classic New York style pizza from the comfort of your own home. Creating this dish is a fun activity that will surely be better than any fast food chain pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball Best Pizza Dough
  • 1/3 cup Easy Pizza Sauce
  • 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 4 oz shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • semolina flour or cornmeal

Steps

  1. Make the pizza dough
  2. Place a pizza stone in the oven and preheat to 500Â°F
  3. Make the pizza sauce
  4. Slice or tear the cheese
  5. Stretch the dough over a dusted pizza peel
  6. Spread the sauce over the stretched dough, making sure to leave enough room for the crust
  7. Place the pizza into the oven for around 5 to 7 minutes, or until the cheese and crust are browned
